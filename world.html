BigBlueButton World 2021

BigBlueButtonWorld 2021 BigBlueButtonWorld was a global conference held from June 21st – 24th for educators, administrators, developers and commercial partners from around the world. With participants from over 20 countries, keynotes and panel discussions surrounding many important themes tailored for each group were held, allowing viewers to engage in dialogue with presenters and panelists.
